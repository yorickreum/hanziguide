Chinese idioms are compact phrases that carry a story, image, or social meaning in just a few Hanzi. Some are classical chengyu used in writing and speeches, while others are everyday sayings, blessing phrases, Hong Kong Cantonese expressions, or modern Mandarin internet slang.
