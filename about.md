## About Hanzi Guide

Hanzi Guide is a free, interactive web application designed to help learners master Chinese character writing through step-by-step stroke order animations and practice exercises.

### Features

**Interactive Character Practice**: Learn to write over 9,000 Chinese characters with animated stroke-by-stroke guidance. Our intuitive interface shows you exactly how each character is written, making it easy to practice proper stroke order and technique.

**Traditional and Simplified Chinese Support**: Seamlessly switch between traditional and simplified Chinese characters using OpenCC conversion technology. Whether you're learning Mandarin, Cantonese, or classical Chinese, Hanzi Guide adapts to your needs.

**Self-Paced Learning**: Practice at your own speed with our character quiz mode. Enter any Chinese character to see its stroke order animation, practice writing it yourself, and get instant visual feedback.

**Mobile-Friendly Design**: Access Hanzi Guide from any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Our responsive design ensures a smooth learning experience wherever you are.

### Why Learn Chinese Characters?

Chinese characters (汉字/漢字) are one of the oldest writing systems still in use today. Learning proper stroke order isn't just about aesthetics - it helps with:

- **Character recognition**: Understanding how characters are constructed
- **Memory retention**: Following stroke order makes characters easier to remember
- **Writing speed**: Proper technique leads to faster, more natural handwriting
- **Cultural understanding**: Connecting with over 1.4 billion Chinese speakers worldwide

### Technology & Open Source

Hanzi Guide is built on open-source technology and freely available character stroke data. The application uses:

- **HanziWriter**: Beautiful stroke order animations
- **Make Me A Hanzi**: Comprehensive character stroke data covering 9,000+ characters
- **OpenCC**: Reliable traditional-simplified Chinese conversion

Visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yorickreum/hanziguide) to contribute, report issues, or learn more about the project.
