Understanding Radicals

Radicals (部首) are key building blocks of Chinese characters. They often tell you something about the meaning or category of a character.

What Radicals Do

Many characters consist of:

  • Radical (部首) – gives meaning or category
  • Phonetic component – hints at pronunciation

Examples of common radicals:

氵water radical ➜ wash, ocean, river
扌hand radical ➜ hit, clap, push
女female radical ➜ mom, sister, younger sister
口mouth radical ➜ tell, drink, eat
亻person radical ➜ stand, rest, act
讠speech radical ➜ say, speak, language
